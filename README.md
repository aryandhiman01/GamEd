## 🚀 Overview

**Gamified Environmental Education Platform** is a web platform designed for **schools and colleges** to promote **environmental awareness, real-world eco-actions, and measurable sustainability impact** through engaging gamification techniques.

Unlike traditional theory-based learning, this platform converts **learning into action**.

---

## ❓ Problem Statement

- Environmental education is mostly **theoretical**
- Students lack **motivation and engagement**
- No system to **measure real environmental impact**
- Minimal connection between learning & real-life action

---

## 💡 Our Solution

A **gamified + AI-driven ecosystem** where students:
- Learn sustainability through games & quizzes
- Perform real-world eco tasks
- Earn points, badges & rankings
- See **actual impact metrics** (CO₂ saved, waste reduced)

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🎮 Gamified Learning
- Interactive quizzes & eco-games  
- Level-based progression system  

### 🏆 Engagement Mechanics
- Badges, streaks & leaderboards  
- Peer-to-peer competition  

### 🌱 Real-World Eco Tasks
- Tree plantation  
- Waste segregation & recycling  
- Energy & water conservation  
- Proof-based task validation  

### 🤖 AI-Powered Personalization
- Adaptive learning paths  
- Smart recommendations based on behavior  

### 📊 Impact Analytics Dashboard
- CO₂ reduction tracking  
- Waste reduction metrics  
- Student & institution-level insights
